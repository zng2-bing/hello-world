Dating Intentions by Age and Gender

How Dating Intentions Shift Across Age and Gender on Dating Apps

Dating apps have grown popular in the past decade. Many have jumped onto the trend with the release of Tinder, Bumble, Hinge, and other dating platforms. However, dating intentions are noticably different across age groups and gender. Among users ages 18–21, casual dating and friendship dominate preferences. At this age, users are likely college students who are still new to romantic relationships and are exploring. By mid 20s to early 30s, long-term relationships and marriage become increasingly common goals for women, signifying that they are ready to settle down. Men in the 20s, however, are still looking more for casual dating and friendships. At ages 30–35, both men and women's goals begin to align more and they both have preferences for long-term relationships and marriage.
